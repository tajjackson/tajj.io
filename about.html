About Me

Elevator Pitch

Hi, my name is Taj Jackson. I am an IT student at Year-Up, and an Application Quality Analyst Intern at Fannie Mae. I am passionate about web development/design, and making your website worth revisiting. I have experience in HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript, and the CMS (Content Management System) Joomla.

I never pictured myself as a web developer. I always thought it was too geeky for me, even though I was a hardcore gamer. However, after completing several projects at Year Up, I found that it was a new opportunity to challenge myself. Barring the sleepless nights and occasional paranoia, I really enjoyed learning how to code and design websites.

Background

Prior to Year-Up, I had no background in coding at all. The only tech experience I had was in Network & Security; I had taken a three month course while in high school for the CCNA (Cisco Certified Network Associate) certification.

What made me switch from Network & Security to web development was the variety of work you can do. Web development is a broad industry with many, many areas to study. I felt that it was where I could grow and express myself the most.
